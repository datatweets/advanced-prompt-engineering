# Module 2 Exercises

## Exercise 1: Audience Insight Table

Collect or write five realistic customer comments. Ask AI to group them into themes.

Required output:

| Segment | Evidence | Pain point | Content need | Confidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Exercise 2: Competitor Comparison

Choose three public competitor descriptions. Paste short excerpts into an AI tool and ask for:

- Target audience.
- Main promise.
- Proof used.
- Content style.
- Unclear claims.
- Possible positioning gap.

## Exercise 3: Persona Cards

Create three role-based persona cards.

Do not use personal names or invented demographic details. Use evidence from the previous exercises.
