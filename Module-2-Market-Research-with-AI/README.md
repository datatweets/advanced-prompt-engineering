# Module 2: Market Research with AI

![Research](https://img.shields.io/badge/focus-market%20research-0891b2)
![Audience](https://img.shields.io/badge/output-audience%20insights-16a34a)

This module shows how to use AI to support market research, audience analysis, competitor review, and customer persona development.

AI can help organize thinking, generate research questions, compare patterns, and turn messy observations into useful summaries. It should not be treated as a replacement for real customer data, current market evidence, or professional judgment.

## Learning Outcomes

After this module, learners should be able to:

1. Write a sharp, specific research question before prompting.
2. Evaluate source material for reliability before using it in a prompt.
3. Ask AI for market hypotheses without presenting them as facts.
4. Analyze competitors using public, verified information with a grounded comparison prompt.
5. Build practical customer personas from evidence and map them to content requirements.

## Module Scenario

A regional service business wants to improve its marketing. The team needs to understand what customers care about, how competitors present themselves, and which content topics should be published first.

## Research Rule

Use AI for structure, questions, synthesis, and drafts. Use real sources for facts.
