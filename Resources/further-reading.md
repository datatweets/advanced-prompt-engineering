# Further Reading

Use this page to collect official documentation, books, articles, and internal policies approved for the course.

## Recommended Reading Areas

| Area | What to look for |
| --- | --- |
| AI basics | Plain English explanations of generative AI and model limits |
| Prompting | Prompt structure, examples, evaluation, and iterative refinement |
| Business use | Case studies on AI-assisted productivity and content workflows |
| Responsible AI | Privacy, bias, transparency, intellectual property, and governance |
| Content strategy | Audience research, channel planning, and measurement |
| Evaluation | Methods for checking quality, accuracy, and usefulness |

## Source Quality Checklist

Prefer sources that:

- Explain methods clearly.
- Separate opinion from evidence.
- Provide publication dates.
- Link to primary sources.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.
- Discuss limitations and risks.

## Add Course Sources

When adding a source, record:

