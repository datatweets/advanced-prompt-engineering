# Project Brief

## Task

Create an AI-assisted content strategy package for a product, service, course, internal initiative, or student project.

Do not use private data. If you use real public sources, record what you used and verify important claims.

## Required Scenario

Choose one:

- A small business launching a new service.
- A corporate department improving an internal workflow.
- A training provider promoting a course.
- A student team explaining a complex topic.
- A nonprofit organization increasing public awareness.

## Required Work

1. Define the audience and goal.
2. Use AI to create research questions.
3. Analyze customer needs or realistic audience notes.
4. Compare at least two competitors, alternatives, or communication examples.
5. Create at least two role-based personas.
6. Draft one long-form content piece.
7. Draft three short-form content pieces.
8. Create one digital storyboard.
9. Build a distribution plan.
10. Review the final package for responsible AI risks.

## Constraints

- Use plain English.
- Do not use personal names in examples.
- Do not invent facts, statistics, quotes, or guarantees.
- Label assumptions clearly.
- Include human review before publication.
