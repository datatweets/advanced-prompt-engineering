# Capstone Project

![Capstone](https://img.shields.io/badge/focus-integrated%20project-2563eb)
![Output](https://img.shields.io/badge/output-strategy%20package-16a34a)

The capstone project combines the full course workflow:

1. Define a business or learning scenario.
2. Conduct AI-assisted audience research.
3. Analyze competitors or alternatives.
4. Create role-based personas.
5. Produce content drafts.
6. Build a distribution plan.
7. Add responsible AI review and team guidelines.

## Final Deliverables

Submit:

- Research summary.
- Persona cards.
- Content strategy.
- One long-form content draft.
- Three short-form content pieces.
- Digital storyboard.
- Distribution plan.
- Responsible AI review checklist.
- Reflection on what changed after review.

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria | Strong submission |
| --- | --- |
| Prompt quality | Prompts include task, context, constraints, format, and review |
| Research discipline | Facts and assumptions are separated |
| Audience fit | Content clearly matches the selected audience |
| Practicality | Outputs can be used or adapted in a real workflow |
| Responsible use | Risks are identified and review steps are included |
| Clarity | Writing is plain, structured, and easy to follow |
