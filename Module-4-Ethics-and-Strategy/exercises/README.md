# Module 4 Exercises

## Exercise 1: Risk Review

Choose one AI-generated draft. Ask AI to review it for:

- Accuracy.
- Privacy.
- Unsupported claims.
- Bias.
- Tone.
- Missing human approval.

## Exercise 2: Distribution Plan

Create a two-week distribution plan for a blog post or campaign.

Required columns:

| Date | Channel | Audience | Message angle | Format | Owner role | Metric |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Exercise 3: AI Usage Guidelines

Write a one-page guideline for a team, business, or classroom.

Include:

- Approved uses.
- Restricted uses.
- Data rules.
- Review checklist.
- Escalation rules.
